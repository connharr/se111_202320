About The Dark Knight

The Dark Knight is the second movie in "The Dark Knight Trilogy" written and directed by Christopher Nolan. The plot of The Dark Knight focuses on the DC Comics hero, Batman, and his allies in their fight agaisnt organized crime in Gothan City.

Director: Christopher Nolan

Release Date: July 14, 2008

Genre: Action, Superhero, Thriller, Crime Film

Rating: PG-13

Running time: 152 minutes
